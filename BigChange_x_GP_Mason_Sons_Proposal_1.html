Create quotes on-site using pre-defined item sets (e.g. CCTV install kit with all line items and pricing). Send to the customer with an acceptance link. Automated follow-up emails when quotes expire — from your GP Mason email, not a generic address. No chasing. No forgetting.

Why BigChange

Not all job management software is the same

There are plenty of options out there. Here's what separates BigChange — specifically for a business like GP Mason with 10 vans, a compliance-heavy workload and plans to grow.

🚐

Vehicle tracking — included, not extra

Most job management tools don't touch vehicle tracking. You'd pay for a separate system on top. BigChange includes hardwired tracking in the licence — we come out, install the cradle in the van, and it's live. With 10 vans, that's a meaningful saving and one less supplier. Travel time between jobs feeds directly into timesheets. ETA alerts go to customers automatically.

⚡

Built for electrical compliance specifically

Generic job management tools give you blank worksheets. BigChange includes a full electrical compliance form pack — EICRs, installation certificates, minor works, emergency lighting, fire alarm certs. Built in, not bolted on. The lads complete them on the tablet, they auto-attach to the job card, and the customer gets them the moment the job closes.

📈

Built to grow — not to be replaced

Some systems work well at 5–6 engineers, then hit a ceiling — functionality, reporting, scale. BigChange runs businesses from 3 engineers to Sunbelt Rentals (2,000+ users) on the same platform. The question isn't just "does it work today" — it's "will it still work when we're twice the size?" BigChange will.

🛠️

24/7 UK support — in Leeds, not outsourced

If one of your lads has a problem on site at 7am, they call a UK number, speak to someone in Leeds, and get it fixed. No ticket, no offshore call centre. Engineers can call BigChange directly from the tablet app. Smaller, newer platforms often mean one or two support people — that's fine until you actually need them on a Monday morning.

Sheffield City Council just renewed 1,500 BigChange licences. BigChange has been operating since 2013. The platform you sign up for today will still be running, still be developing, and still be supporting GP Mason in 3 years — and beyond.
